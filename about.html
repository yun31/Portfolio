My Story

Hi, I’m Yunseo Choi, a Human-Computer Interaction researcher and designer. My journey started in Human Resource Development, where I explored how individuals learn, grow, and thrive. This foundation, combined with a second major in Information Science, has shaped my interdisciplinary approach to designing intuitive, human-centered systems.

My work focuses on creating meaningful interactions that empower individuals and make technology more accessible, creative, and inclusive. From exploring how large language models can support self-reflection and decision-making to designing immersive VR experiences, I thrive at the intersection of innovation and empathy.

Currently, I’m particularly interested in human-AI collaboration, and spatial computing—technologies that have the power to redefine how we connect, learn, and grow. Whether it’s helping job seekers overcome anxiety through VR mock interviews or designing eco-friendly digital tools, my goal is to create solutions that enhance the human experience while addressing real-world challenges.
